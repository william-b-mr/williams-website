About me

With over 7 years of international experience across semiconductor factories, aircraft assembly lines and IT systems, I have seen firsthand how the power of collective intelligence enables us to achieve extraordinary feats. I’ve built expertise in LEAN methodologies, helping companies streamline operations, reduce waste, and maximize value for the customer. I am constantly amazed by what humanity can achieve when we combine our expertise, creativity, and technology.

My passion lies in using this combination of science, technology, and human collaboration to solve complex challenges, create economic value, and drive progress. Whether optimizing manufacturing processes or integrating advanced technologies like AI and data analytics, my goal is always to unlock new levels of efficiency and innovation. I believe that by working together—leveraging the best of our technical skills and human ingenuity—we can build more resilient, sustainable, and impactful industries.

Together with my engineering background, I've gathered advanced analytics skills (Python, SQL, Advanced Statistics and ML) and cloud computing knowledge (AWS + GCP certifications) through 500+ hours of self-learning and post-graduate studies.

I aspire to establish my own small farm in the future, with a strong belief in the transformative potential of rural areas, a vision I intend to actualize through a project named "Nowhere Desk."

My endeavours have taken me to work in Portugal, Germany, the Netherlands, Taiwan, South Korea and the USA. Additionally, I spent a gap year in Brazil.

I feel at home in the Ocean. I kitesurf, (scuba)dive and swim on a regular basis.
