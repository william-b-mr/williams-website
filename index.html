About me

Emerging from the heart of the semiconductor industry, where I contributed significantly to the manufacturing of EUV machines driving chip technology, I'm now embarking on an exciting journey into the world of cloud and data. While at ASML, I witnessed firsthand how EUV machines produced the very chips powering cloud and AI innovations. Today, I bring this unique perspective to the cloud, equipped with certifications in AWS Solutions Architecture and GCP Cloud Engineering. I bridge the gap between semiconductor excellence and the limitless possibilities of cloud and data, helping businesses thrive in the digital era.
